# Product Overview

Karanova is an AI-powered business management platform targeting the Iranian market with bilingual support (Persian/English).

## Core Modules

1. **Inova** - AI Business Intelligence with 4 specialized agents:
   - Vision AI (strategic planning)
   - Govern AI (compliance & governance)
   - Supply AI (supply chain optimization)
   - Creative AI (content & marketing)

2. **TaskEase** - AI-integrated project management with organization-based hierarchy

3. **BIQ (Dashboard)** - Business health monitoring with KPIs and real-time metrics

## Key Value Propositions

- 60% reduction in project management time
- 24/7 AI-powered assistance
- Real-time collaboration and analytics
- Multi-tenant architecture with organization isolation

## Target Audience

Entrepreneurs, business owners, project managers, and investors in Iran seeking to streamline operations and leverage AI for business intelligence.

## Brand Identity

- **Colors**: Teal (hsl(177, 70%, 40%)) and Cyan (hsl(185, 85%, 45%))
- **Tone**: Professional, sleek, modern, production-ready
- **Language**: Persian content, English communication with developers
- **Design**: Fresh, responsive, with glass morphism and glow effects

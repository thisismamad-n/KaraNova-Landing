# Product Overview

Karanova is an AI-powered business management platform designed for the Iranian market with international support. The landing page showcases three core modules through creative animations and bilingual content.

## Core Modules

1. **Inova** - AI Business Intelligence with 4 specialized agents (Vision AI, Govern AI, Supply AI, Creative AI)
2. **TaskEase** - AI-integrated project management with organization-based hierarchy
3. **BIQ (Dashboard)** - Business health monitoring with KPIs and metrics

## Key Value Propositions

- 60% reduction in project management time
- 24/7 AI-powered assistance
- RTL support for Persian/Farsi language
- Real-time collaboration and analytics
- Multi-tenant architecture with organization isolation

## Target Audience

Entrepreneurs, business owners, project managers, and investors in Iran seeking to streamline operations and leverage AI for business intelligence.

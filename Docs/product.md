# Product Overview

Karanova is an AI-powered business management platform designed for entrepreneurs, business owners, and investors in Iran (with international support).

## Three Core Modules

1. **Inova** - AI Business Intelligence with 4 specialized agents:
   - Vision AI: Market analysis, trend forecasting, opportunity identification
   - Govern AI: Legal compliance, regulations, risk management, investment consulting
   - Supply AI: Supply chain optimization, customer support, networking
   - Creative AI: Content creation, creative problem-solving, document analysis/editing

2. **TaskEase** - AI-integrated project management:
   - Organization-based hierarchy (Organizations → Teams → Projects → Tasks)
   - Role-based access control with granular permissions
   - Sprint management, task dependencies, timeline visualization
   - AI-assisted team building and task creation
   - Kanban boards, burndown charts, velocity tracking

3. **BIQ (Dashboard)** - Business health monitoring:
   - Business Health Check (BHC) metrics and KPIs
   - Health indicators for different business sections
   - Calendar integration and personal notes
   - Overview of TaskEase and Inova activities

## Key Features

- RTL (right-to-left) support for Persian/Farsi language
- Global AI chatbot toggle accessible throughout the app
- Multi-tenant architecture with organization isolation
- Real-time collaboration and notifications
- Telegram bot integration for external access
